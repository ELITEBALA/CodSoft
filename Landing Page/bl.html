About Us

  • Joystick Junction Eatery is a unique destination that seamlessly blends the excitement of retro gaming with the delight of exceptional dining. Step into our vibrant space, adorned with nostalgic arcade decor and a lively atmosphere that captures the essence of gaming culture.
  • Join us for a meal, host your gaming events, or simply hang out with friends over a round of games and delicious eats. At Joystick Junction Eatery, every moment is a play, every taste is an adventure.
  • As you explore our arcade section, you'll discover a treasure trove of classic and modern games, from arcade cabinets to interactive experiences that cater to gamers of all ages. Engage in friendly competitions, relive childhood memories, or discover new favorites while immersing yourself in the world of gaming entertainment.
  • When it comes to dining, Joystick Junction takes your taste buds on an unforgettable journey. Our menu features a delectable selection of dishes crafted with quality ingredients and culinary expertise. From savory appetizers to mouthwatering mains and indulgent desserts, each bite is a symphony of flavors designed to elevate your dining experience.
  • Whether you're here for a casual meal, a celebratory gathering, or a gaming extravaganza, Joystick Junction Eatery offers a captivating fusion of entertainment and gastronomy, making every visit a memorable adventure for you and your fellow gamers.

Events

Game Night Tournament

Join us for an adrenaline-pumping Game Night Tournament! Compete against fellow gamers in exciting challenges and multiplayer showdowns. Test your skills, strategize your moves, and vie for the top spot on our leaderboard. Prizes, bragging rights, and epic gaming moments await!

Cosplay Contest

Unleash your inner hero, villain, or gaming character at our Cosplay Contest! Dress up as your favorite gaming icons, superheroes, or fantasy creatures and showcase your creativity and craftsmanship. Strike a pose, wow the crowd, and stand a chance to win prizes and recognition for your awesome cosplay!

Live Music Nights

Get ready to rock and roll at our Live Music Nights! Enjoy electrifying performances by local bands, solo artists, and DJs as they amp up the atmosphere with tunes that complement your gaming and dining experience. Dance, sing along, and groove to the rhythm of live music!

Trivia Tuesdays

Put your knowledge to the test at Trivia Tuesdays! Gather your friends, form a team, and compete in a fun-filled trivia night covering a range of topics from pop culture to gaming history. Win prizes, earn bragging rights, and enjoy an evening of brain-teasing challenges and laughter!
